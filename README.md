# Customer_Churn_Analysis
## Overview

Customer retention is essential for businesses because retaining existing customers is often more cost-effective than acquiring new ones. In this project, I performed an exploratory data analysis (EDA) on a telecommunications customer dataset to identify the characteristics associated with customer churn and uncover insights that could help improve customer retention.

## Objectives

The analysis focuses on answering the following business questions:

* Which customers are most likely to churn?
* How do customer charges relate to churn?
* Do international and voicemail plans influence customer retention?
* Is there a relationship between customer service calls and churn?
* What business actions can be taken to reduce customer churn?

## Project Highlights

* Performed data exploration and statistical analysis to compare churned and non-churned customers.
* Created a **Total Charges** feature by combining day, evening, night, and international charges to better analyze customer spending.
* Explored the relationship between churn and customer characteristics, including monthly charges, call duration, customer service calls, international plans, and voicemail plans.
* Identified patterns associated with customer churn and translated the findings into actionable business recommendations.
